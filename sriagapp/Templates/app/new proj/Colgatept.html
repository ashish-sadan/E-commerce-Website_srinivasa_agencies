OVERVIEW

William Colgate started Colgate-Palmolive in 1806 as a small soap and candle business in New York City. The company has since grown to serve billions of people worldwide. Here are some of the company's milestones: 1820: Colgate opens a starch factory in Jersey City, New Jersey 1872: The Peet brothers start a soap company in Kansas City, Kansas that makes Crystal White soap 1873: Colgate develops its first toothpaste, which is sold in a jar 1896: Colgate introduces tubes 1950s: Colgate becomes popular with the slogan "It Cleans Your Breath While It Cleans Your Teeth"
